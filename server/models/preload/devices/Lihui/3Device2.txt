Biosensor-LuxR-3OC6-HSL or LasR-3OC12-HSL complexes 2014ETH_Zurich
This device responds to LuxR protein and produce Bxb protein afterwards.
The LuxR-3OC6-HSL or LasR-3OC12-HSL complexes bind to the corresponding promoters Plux and Plas on the second plasmid and induce the expression of two integrases Bxb1 or ¦µC31, respectively.
